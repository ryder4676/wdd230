White Space & Clean Design

Shopify

Shopify.ca

The Shopify website is a good example of Whitespace and clean design. When you look at it you will notice that there is not alot of information in any space. The whitespace helps give the elements a clean looking design.

Proximity

Alberta Treasury Branch

atb.com

ATB's website is a great example of how each set of elements are placed closer together if they are related, and further apart if they are unrelated.

Repetition

Amazon

Amazon.ca

As you scroll down you will notice a certain format in adds. Often there are 4 ads grouped together in a quad ad group. These only get devided by single ads. Then everything repeats in these type of ads.
